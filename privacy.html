Introduction

Your privacy is important to us at BeingTraveler ("BeingTraveler" or "we"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the personal information you share with us through our website or mobile application (collectively, the "Platform"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the Platform. By using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

The personal information we gather is used to improve our services, to enable certain functions, to respond to queries, to contact you, and otherwise to serve you better. We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and address.
  • Information related to your travel plans and bookings, such as details of services booked, travel dates, timings, locations, etc.
  • Payment and transaction information used to process payments and prevent fraud.
  • Information generated through your use of the Platform, such as IP address, browser type, device ID, location, clicks, etc.

How We Use and Share Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following business purposes:

  • To provide the services you request through the Platform, such as booking travel services on your behalf.
  • To respond to your inquiries and communicate with you.
  • To provide you with alerts and notices related to your bookings and travel plans.
  • For marketing purposes, where you have consented to receive marketing communications from us. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • We may share your personal information with third parties essential to providing our services, such as travel service providers, payment gateways, marketing agencies, etc. We ensure, through contractual means, that any third party who receives your personal information respects the security and safety of your data.
  • We may disclose your personal information in response to official requests legally filed by law enforcement or legal agencies, or in cases where we have reason to believe that sharing such information is necessary to report illegal activity or prevent injury or financial loss.

How We Protect Your Information

  • We follow generally accepted standards to store and protect the personal information you provide, both during transmission and once it is received.
  • We use firewalls, encryption, restricted access, and other procedural safeguards to prevent unauthorized access to your information.
  • We authorize access to personal information only for those individuals and agencies necessary to perform their legitimate job functions.
  • We retain your travel booking information for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required by law.
